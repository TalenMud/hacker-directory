Metropolis–Hastings, live

A little demo of what you can build on your own page. This is a real Metropolis–Hastings sampler exploring a 2D mixture-of-Gaussians target distribution — the walker proposes a random step, accepts it with probability min(1, p(x')/p(x)), and rejects otherwise. Warmer cells in the heatmap mean the chain has spent more time there, which is exactly where the true density is highest.
